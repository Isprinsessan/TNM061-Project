    Background Story

  • Dr. Engelbrekt was an evil nazi doctor, who made experiments on poor animals. Mandeln was a small armadillo who Dr. Engelbrekt found in the woods. When he was experimented on, his brain evolved into one which is more adapted than a human's. He managed to escape from his cage, and now he's trying to esacpe from the evil doctor's factory.
